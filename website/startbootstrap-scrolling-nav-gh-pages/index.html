About Linguists Without Borders


Studies estimate that over half of the world's seven thousand languages will likely go extinct by 2100. Unfortunately, there is a severe shortage of trained linguists with the expertise to document these endangered tongues before they vanish irrevocably. In this project, supported by an MIT IDEAS Global Challenge Grant, we propose the development of a mobile application that provide elicitation templates for probing the syntactic features of a language, generates dictionaries, an offers a convenient way to record and store proverbs, songs, and stories. Our hope is that this light-weight software tool will empower communities to document and preserve their own linguistic legacies, while curating a collective global repository of irreplaceable linguistic and cultural data.
