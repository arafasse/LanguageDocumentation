Mission


Studies estimate that over half of the world's seven thousand languages will likely go extinct by 2100. Unfortunately, there is a severe shortage of trained linguists with the expertise to document these endangered tongues before they vanish irrevocably. Linguists Without Borders provides training resources and digital tools for language documentation, as well as an online repository of linguistic and cultural knowledge. Our hope is that these tools will empower communities to document and preserve their own linguistic legacies, while curating a collective global repository of irreplaceable linguistic and cultural data. LWB aims to democratize the process of language documentation, catalyzing a paradigm shift in the field away from a limited cohort of professional linguists and towards scalable, public, crowd-sourced data collection.

Our Technology


The LWB app constitutes just a small portion of our overall mission, but it is the original innovation and still lies at the heart of our ... mission: empowering everyone, regardless of ..., to crux of our innovation and ... An untrained user interacting with a linguistic community through the app will be prompted to elicit utterances in the target language. The resultant audio footage will be recorded, compressed, and stored in a local database, which can then be migrated to centralized servers (once an internet connection is available) for long-term storage and dissemination. The vocabulary words selected by comparative linguists on the basis of their universality. The user is free to generate her own custom vocabulary lists as well (which can be uploaded in several formats directly to the app). Grammatical templates will then prompt the user to ask for translations of specific utterances, which will illuminate certain syntactic features of the language (such as word order, plural formation, morphological markings, etc.). The app also provides the user with the opportunity record “free-form” audio, thereby collecting songs, stories, proverbs, and other unstructured linguistic data.
